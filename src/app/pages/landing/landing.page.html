About Campus Market

Campus Market is a student-to-student marketplace designed specifically for university communities. We connect buyers and sellers within your campus, making it easy to trade textbooks, electronics, furniture, and everything else you need for student life.

Our platform ensures safe transactions between verified students, with built-in messaging, payment tracking, and order management. Whether you're looking to save money on textbooks or make some cash selling items you no longer need, Campus Market is your trusted marketplace.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a seller?
Sign up, complete your profile, then apply to become a seller from your dashboard. Admin will review and approve your application within 24 hours.
Is payment secure?
Yes! We support multiple payment methods including Cash on Delivery, GCash, and Bank Transfer. All payments are verified before orders are processed.
How do I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" in your dashboard to see real-time status updates. You'll also receive notifications for every status change.
